Common Issues & Solutions
🔍 Certificate Not Found
Ensure the certificate ID is correct and try refreshing the page. Contact your institution if the issue persists.
🔐 Login Issues
Clear browser cache and cookies, or use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your credentials.
📄 PDF Upload Problems
Ensure your file is in PDF format and under 1MB. Try using a different browser if the issue continues.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I verify a certificate?
Go to our verification portal and either upload the PDF certificate or enter the certificate details manually.
Can I download my verified certificate?
Yes, after successful verification, you can download the certificate PDF and share it to DigiLocker.
What if my certificate shows as invalid?
Contact our support team immediately with your certificate details. This could indicate a security issue.
How secure are blockchain certificates?
Our certificates are secured using blockchain technology, making them tamper-proof and permanently verifiable.
